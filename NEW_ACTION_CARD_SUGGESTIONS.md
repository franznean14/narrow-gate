# New Action Card Suggestions for Lampstand

## Current Action Cards:
- Shield of Faith (Defuse Stumble)
- Encouragement (Save friend OR Remove Burden)
- Insight (Peek at deck)
- Guidance (Shuffle deck)
- Patience (Delay threats)
- Modesty (Skip turn, next draws 2)
- Kindness (Give card)
- Imitate Faith (Copy buff)
- Days Cut Short (End Tribulation)
- Love (Heal Unity)

## Mechanics to Consider:
- Cards that interact with Unity level
- Cards that manipulate discard pile
